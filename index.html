Bio

I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. (M.S./Ph.D. integrated course) student at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Seon Joo Kim. My research interests focus on Computational Photography and Image/Video Understanding. Particularly, I'm interested in Deep Learning and its applications to Image Filtering/Generation.
I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science at Yonsei University, in 2014. Also, I have three years of work experience developing android/server-side applications at two startup companies.
